Football Analytics · Machine Learning

In-play match outcome prediction from live signals.

LiveMatch Edge combines match-clock statistics, bookmaker odds, and supervised learning to estimate final football outcomes during play and evaluate confidence-based decision rules.

What the workflow covers

The notebook keeps the modeling flow transparent: feature engineering, chronological evaluation, model comparison, and simulated live decisions are kept in one reproducible path.

Live feature construction

Minute-level statistics are ordered by fixture and match clock, then converted into model-ready signals.

Market probability features

Decimal odds are normalized into implied probabilities for home win, draw, and away win outcomes.

Strategy evaluation

Model confidence is tested with threshold and cutoff-minute rules to study decision quality.
